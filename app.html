Hand Controls

Right Hand:
• Move hand to position the effect
• Pinch (thumb to index) to control size
• Rotate wrist/thumb to control rotation
Left Hand:
• Move left/right to change colors
• Movement speed controls energy level
Keyboard:
• Press SPACE to change effects
• Press 'D' to toggle debug info
• Press 'R' to toggle recording
Recording:
• Click the "Start Recording" button or press 'R'
• Recording will save as MP4 when supported by your browser
• Otherwise saves as WebM that can be converted online

Camera Access Required

This application needs access to your camera to track your hands.
